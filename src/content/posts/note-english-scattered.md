## About IN ON AT

### For TIME

**in → longer or general periods**
- *in* 2026, *in* February, *in* winter, *in* the morning
- *in* a few minutes, *in* two weeks

**on → specific days or dates**
- *on* Tuesday, *on* February 17, *on* my birthday, *on* New Year's Day

**at → exact times**
- *at* 3:00 PM, *at* noon, *at* night, *at* the moment

### For PLACE

**in → inside an area, city, country, or enclosed space**
- *in* China, *in* Beijing, *in* the room, *in* the park

**on → on a surface, street, or specific transport (like a bus/train)**
- *on* the table, *on* Wall Street, *on* the bus, *on* the floor

**at → a specific point or location**
- *at* home, *at* the station, *at* school, *at* the door

### Quick Examples

1. I'll see you on Tuesday <span class="hide-text">on</span> Tuesday <span class="hide-text">at</span> 5 PM <span class="hide-text">in</span>​ the café.

### Memory Aid
- **in**​ = big box (time/place)
- **on**​ = surface/day    
- **at**​ = dot/point

**Scope: *in* > *on* > *at***
